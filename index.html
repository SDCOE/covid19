Last updated: 03/04/2020

In response to the global spread of COVID-19 Coronavirus, several technology companies are providing free access to additional features and capabilities of their online tools for a limited time.

Microsoft Teams - Office 365 E1

Free 6 month access to a premium tier of Office 365 E1 including Microsoft Teams

Google Hangouts Meet

Free access through July 1 to advanced Hangouts Meet video-conferencing capabilities to all G-Suite & G-Suite for Education customers
- Larger meetings - up to 250 participants per call
- Live streaming up to 100,000 viewers
- Record meetings and save to Google Drive

Cisco Webex

Free Webex access to additional features:
- Unlimited usage (no time restrictions)
- Supports up to 100 participants
- Offers toll dial-in (in addition to existing VoIP capabilities

GoToMeeting - LogMeIn

Free Emergency Remote Work Kits for 3 months
- Solutions for meetings and video conferencing
- Webinars and virtual events
- IT support & management of remote devices and apps 

Zoom

Advanced capabilities with free access to audio and video conferencing tools:
- Unlimited number of video meetings with up to 100 participants
- Breakout rooms
- Collaboration tools with other teachers

Kahoot! - Online Challenges & Educational Games

Free access to Kahoot! Premium:
- Advanced reporting
- School-wide educational games
- Collaboration tools with other teachers
